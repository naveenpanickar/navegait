About navegait

Built for the workforce coding platforms ignore.

Traditional job boards rely on resumes. Coding platforms validate engineers. But millions of professionals now work through specialised business technologies — Figma, Webflow, HubSpot, Salesforce, Power BI, Shopify, GA4 — and these roles remain structurally underserved by hiring infrastructure.

navegait creates a dedicated ecosystem: Platform Skills Intelligence Network™ — combining requirement intelligence, candidate discovery, hands-on assessments, SME review panels and AI-powered fitment scoring.
