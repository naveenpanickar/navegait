Why NAVEGAIT

Partner ecosystems are hard to see, govern and scale

Every enterprise depends on external partners to deliver — yet most run that web of relationships on spreadsheets, inboxes and tribal knowledge. That gap slows delivery, hides risk, and shuts good SME partners out of work they're capable of doing.

Scattered information

Partner data lives across emails, spreadsheets and siloed systems.

Inconsistent governance

Onboarding and compliance vary partner by partner, team by team.

Limited visibility

Little real-time insight into performance, cost or outcomes.

SMEs left behind

Good partners struggle to become enterprise-ready and get discovered.

What we're building

A managed service, wrapped in a platform

NAVEGAIT EPX orchestrates the full partner lifecycle — discover, evaluate, onboard, execute, measure, grow — through one connected ecosystem of people, process, data, technology and trust. It's not just software: a dedicated Partner Office runs alongside the platform.

01

A real Partner Office

A dedicated team runs discovery, onboarding and governance — not a ticket queue.

02

A lightweight workspace

Enterprises and partners share one system of record instead of scattered files.

03

Governance by design

Compliance, risk and approvals are built into every workflow, not bolted on.

04

Data that compounds

Every interaction feeds analytics that make the next match, audit and decision faster.

  • Deeper integrations with enterprise systems
