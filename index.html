Three Core Partnering Domains

navegait EPX is purpose-built around three enterprise domains where partnering intent is high and execution is consistently under-structured. Every ThINQ' blueprint draws from a curated partner intelligence repository — so each engagement is sharper and faster than the last.

01 — INK Workstream Partners

Print · Signage · Document

Enterprise INK workstreams — managed print, signage, labels, packaging, and document management — represent a consistent but fragmented spend category. navegait EPX maps the right partner ecosystem to consolidate, optimise, and govern these workstreams at scale.

Managed Print Services

Fleet optimisation, consumables management, and cost-per-page models for enterprise print.

Signage & Large Format

Indoor, outdoor, and digital signage partners for retail, campus, and corporate environments.

Labels & Packaging

Specialist label, barcode, and product packaging partners across manufacturing and logistics.

Document Management

Digitisation, archiving, workflow automation, and compliance-grade document partners.

02 — ITAM Partners

Hardware & Software

IT asset management spans the full hardware and software lifecycle — procurement strategy, refresh cycles, disposition, and compliance. Executing this well requires alignment across vendors, resellers, ITAD, licensing, and sustainability workstreams simultaneously.

HW Procurement & Resellers

OEM-aligned and multi-brand resellers covering device, server, and infrastructure supply.

SW Licensing & SAM

Software asset management, licence optimisation, and compliance advisory partners.

ITAD & Lifecycle

Certified IT asset disposition, data sanitisation, refurbishment, and end-of-life logistics.

Asset Finance & Refresh

Technology refresh, leasing, and circular economy models that reduce capex exposure.

03 — CSR / ESG Partners

Impact & Compliance

Enterprise CSR and ESG commitments are now board-level obligations — tied to procurement, operations, and mandatory disclosure. Executing them requires a structured ecosystem of impact, reporting, and delivery partners, not a single vendor relationship.

NGO & Social Impact

Mission-aligned delivery partners for community, inclusion, and social value workstreams.

ESG Reporting & Assurance

Frameworks, measurement, and third-party assurance partners for sustainability disclosure.

Circularity & Waste

Partners enabling product circularity, e-waste compliance, and environmental accountability.

Skilling & Livelihood

Training, certification, and employment pathway partners supporting CSR mandate delivery.
